Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

...read more

You can login with your HZ account username and password at print.hz.nl and upload the file you want to print. With your HZ studentpass you'll have access to any HZ printer and print your uploaded files.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

...read more

Scan your pass at a HZ printer, put the document you want to scan in the printer, and select scan on the printer. The files will be send to your HZ mail box.

How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

...read more

To access the webshop you'll need to login with your HZ account at webshop.hz.nl , select what you want, choose your bank and pay with the online banking system.

Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick?

...read more

You can find the latest updates on Covid on the Corona FAQpage on the hz website. When you're sick you have to report to your study programme coordinator and or the helpdesk on the ground floor. When you're at home you can follow the lectures online.

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

...read more

Login on to the my HZ portal, in your personal menu you can click on Reservations & visitors. On this page you'll be able to book a room.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

...read more

Free parking is possible on the car park of PZEM at the Poelendaelesingel 10 in Middelburg. This is a few minutes' walk to the HZ. Unfortunately, it is not possible to park directly at the HZ building.
However, there are two disabled parking spaces and spaces for HZ cars near the building itself.

Please note: if you park at the Poelendaelesingel, you cannot reach the HZ via the tunnel because of the construction works for the Joint Research Center Zeeland. You can cross over via the intersection at the traffic lights.
