Study Choice

Should she Stay or Should she Go? Steady, As She doesn't go, There She Goes

Study choice activities

For ICT of HZ University I went to the open day and have participated in the student-for-a-day program. I've done the same for the bachelor 'Bouwkunde' of HZ University and I've participated in various online open days, while I was a fourth and fifth year in Highschool, Art Schools and to orientate with other studies.

Motivate your study choice

I've chosen to study ICT at HZ University mostly to gain enough study points, in hopefully the first year, to have the choice to go study English Language and Culture at the University of Utrecht. However I have also taken a liking in Game development at BUAS.

The second would be obvious to switch to after ICT at HZ, and is my first choice to switch to if I get accepted. I do take a lot of interest in programming, digital design, game develepment and the English Language.
The latter being why I, as a native Dutch speaker, prefer to be placed in the international class when that's possible.

I chose this course not only to learn the basics of programming for a first year ICT, but also because of the block focussing on game development.
I am highly motivated for the upcoming school year and ready as ever, after a gap year, to start studying again.

After completing the study

As I stated above, I'm planning on switching after my first year or after gaining the required amount of study points.

I'm not centain yet which exact profession I'll end up loving, but I am sure I either want a job in the film industry, for either screan writing or related to the digital design, or in publising, considering my hobby of reading and writing for fun.

During the study, I expect to learn programming and gain a sufficient amount of knowledge about game development.
Furthermore I'd like my English language to improve, being surrounded by international students. And I am exited to get to know a lot more of people and make great friends the following year!
