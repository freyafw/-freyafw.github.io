Hi Freya,

Welcome to our program!
So your in for the first year and continue on to University. Although we did not have many students who have done that with us. Keep in mind that it might be challenging to keep your motivation up in group work knowing you want to continue on to university and the rest of the group members have different ideas about their study (or lack motivation). So I would advise you to align with motivated students in a group as to achieve study succes.

I would also advice to gain knowledge on both fields. English study versus media ICT so you can make a well informed decision which way you would like to go.

We do not offer much wihtin the creative Media game technologies in our program. So maybe it would also make sense to check the college program in Breda, I believe, which is more focused on ICT and media than we are. Just keep in mind that most of our program is about coding.
We do offer the international program and we are going to build a game so two of the elements you are looking for will be given.

Lastly having your study plan set I can also advise you to sign yourself up at the beginning of the schoolyear for the IT personality project weeks since those study point are often forgotten and you are in for obtaining your propedeutic certificate in the first year.
Having a set plan in place and remaining disciplined is what will help your though!
